Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Regardless of latitude, cloudiness and windiness is difficult to conclude a hypothesis because of the distribution. Though, most cities are less windy, there isn’t a correlation between wind and latitude because cities seem fairly evenly distributed. The main determining factor here is the correlation between latitude and temperature. Cloudiness and windiness don’t seem to affect temperature by much, else you’d see a different plot.
