Latitude vs. Humidity

Cities that are humid are very humid and dry cities are very dry. There is a concentration of cities at the upper (humidity) part of the graph, which illustrates that the majority of cities are humid, but looking at the lower part, few cities are very dry. These cities are evenly distributed enough at latitude to make a conclusion about the correlation between humidity and latitude, but what we can say is that the majority of cities are humid and that the drier cities are very dry.
